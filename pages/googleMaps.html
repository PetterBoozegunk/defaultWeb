Google maps

This plugin (or feature) shows one ore more (google) maps on a page.

googleMaps.js uses "spin.js" and "spinner.js"

These files are loaded by "googleMaps.js":

  1. https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/js
  2. http://google-maps-utility-library-v3.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/markerclusterer/src/markerclusterer.js
